üéØ MAIN FEATURE: Visitor Teleportation During Slot Switch

I implemented automatic visitor detection and teleportation when a player
switches between island slots. This prevents visitors from being left
behind on an island that is being swapped out.

How it works:
1. Player initiates a slot switch (as before)
2. System detects all visitors (non-team members) on the island
3. Visitors receive a warning message
4. After 2 seconds (configurable), visitors are teleported away
5. Visitors with their own island ‚Üí teleported home
6. Visitors without an island ‚Üí teleported to world spawn

Key Points:
- ‚úÖ Team members are NOT treated as visitors (they stay with the island)
- ‚úÖ Warning message gives visitors time to understand what's happening
- ‚úÖ Delay is configurable (default: 40 ticks = 2 seconds)
- ‚úÖ Smart destination selection (home vs spawn)
- ‚úÖ Clear notifications throughout the process

CONFIGURATION OPTIONS
=================================================

Edit config.yml to customize behavior:

```yaml

  # Delay before teleporting (in ticks, 20 = 1 second)
  teleport-delay: 40

  # Warning message shown to visitors
  # Set to "" to disable warning
  warning-message: "&eThe island owner is switching islands. You will be teleported in 2 seconds..."
```

Test these by:
1. Changing teleport-delay to 100 (5 seconds)
2. Changing warning-message to custom text
